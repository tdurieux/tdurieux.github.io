# \Bluelatex

\BlueLaTeX aims to provide a tool chain to easily write documents collaboratively. The first intended audience of \BlueLaTeX is the academic world (for now!), by integrating concepts and lifecycles particularly well-suited for these kind of documents, but it can be used for any other kind of document.
